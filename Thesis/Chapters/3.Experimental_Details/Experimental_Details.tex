\section{Area Fraction Analysis}
Area fraction is given by:
\begin{equation}
V_{v} = \dfrac{\sum A_{A}}{A_{T}}
\end{equation} 

Error is given by:
\begin{equation}
E_{A}^{2} = \dfrac{1}{N}\left[ 1+ \left( \dfrac{\sigma}{\overline A} \right)^{2} \right]
\end{equation}
